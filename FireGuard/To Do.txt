1. Get the video stream
	1.1 Get the video stream from the drone to a computer or server.

2. Analyse the video stream
	2.1. Use ml5js or the CV2 libary to analyse the video stream for fire and other objects / indications of fire / dangers.

3. If fire or another danger is detected:
	3.1. Send the drones coordinates.
	3.2. Send a image to the drone operator.
	3.3. Alert the drone operator.

4. Path
	4.1 Create a flight path
	4.2 Make the drone able to stop and circle the fire
